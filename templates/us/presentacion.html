Group presentation

The Radio Frequency Group (GRF) is one of the research groups of the the Department of Signal Theory and Communications of the Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. GRF was formally created in 2005 although i was recognized by Universidad Carlos III de Madrid since 1997.

GRF members have specialized knowledge and experience on different areas related to high frequency technologies, including computational electromagnetics, microwave engineering and antennas. This knowledge allows GRF to lead a number of international and national research projects, and to provide integral consulting solutions to the industry.
